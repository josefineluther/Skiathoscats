SKIATHOS Cat Welfare Association

The small charity doing big work

Skiathos Cat Welfare Association (SCWA), is a non profit organisation providing veterinary care and food to the stray cats of Skiathos. Everyday we feed around 300 cats in our shelter and around Skiathos.

Read more about our work

Ways to help

Are you interested in helping our cats? You can help through donations, volonteering at our shelter or by adoption. You can also help us with feeding stray-cats. Read more about how to help below.
