FEEDING
STRAY-CATS

Skiathos is full of cats that need food and water. We encourage you to feed the cats, but be sure you do it in the right way. Here’s som tips and tricks:

  • Try not to feed the stray-cats when you are eating at tavernas. Feed the cats with dry cat-food away from the taverna.

  • Use a plastic container to put the food in. Wait for the cat to eat and remove the container. Leaving food on the ground, leaves the cats and those that feed them subject to complaints.

  • Provide water for the strays, they suffer from lack of water as much as from lack of food.

  • Please do not let cats enter your hotel room. Remember not everybody likes cats and some people might be allergic.
